What are Assistants?

Assistants are here to help. Instead of going directly to a consultant or bank, Novella’s smart assistants act as an intermediate step to help save you time and resources. Each assistant will inquire about your needs and provide proffessional advice based on your goals and your current financial position. Please be adviced that these assistants will only provide an estimate as to what steps to take or additional financial advice.
